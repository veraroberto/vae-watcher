VAE Watcher

Welcome to VAE Watcher!

VAE Watcher is the result of our team wanting to provide a thorough analysis on the progress of COVID vaccination in the US, more specifically, the VAEs (Vaccine Adverse Events) registered. We analyzed the data obtained by the VAE Report System, to determine key factors that influence VAEs in people of all ages. We want to combat the misinformation and fear associated with getting the vaccine, by providing you with real data provided by real people.

Our results were obtained through the use of machine learning technologies to ensure the data is as clean and as accurate as possible. Linear regression and text analysis were some of the methods used to complete this project.
